How to play

  • Tap or click a non-path cell to place the selected turret.
  • Turrets earn XP from kills (40 % to the killer, 30 % to the closest turret, 30 % split evenly). Each level boosts damage, range, fire rate, and splash — with diminishing returns.
  • The digit inside the turret is its current level.
  • Enemies pick a random path each spawn. Each map is the same for everyone today.
